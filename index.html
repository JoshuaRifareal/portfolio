I am fascinated with how a creative outlook in life makes everything become suddenly interesting – the sunrise, a simple coffee in the morning, some nice cozy song, conversations with people, and all those little things – inspiring me to stay creative and dream more in the pursuit of great ideas. This might sound a bit over-dramatic (well yeah) but honestly, it’s how I learned to love graphic design. It allowed me to express myself and turn a somewhat dull canvas, into something more pleasing, yet functional piece of work.
(now, enough with the drama lol)

During high school, I have been the layout artist in the few school clubs and organizations I became part of. I designed t-shirts, banners, logos, posters and many other similar projects which allowed me to not only explore digital art, but realize the importance of connecting to people through collaborative works.
